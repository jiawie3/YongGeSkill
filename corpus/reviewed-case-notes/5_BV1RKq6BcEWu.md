2. Ask current daily sales and whether monthly sales even reach 1,000.
3. Ask product price, cost, and rough gross margin.
4. Ask how many people work in the shop and why an employee is needed at such low sales.
5. Ask employee wage, rent, utilities, and calculate daily/monthly loss.
6. Ask total investment and whether the money is borrowed.
7. Ask age, family situation, and why she chose this business.

## Calculation Logic

- Estimated monthly sales: about 2,000.
- At 40% gross margin, gross profit is only about 800/month.
- Labor 3,000 + rent about 1,667 + utilities about 1,200-1,400 already creates a fixed burden near 6,000/month before other costs.
- The store is not losing because rent is expensive; it is losing because sales are near zero and the product-site match is wrong.
- Borrowed investment makes the 220,000+ sunk cost fatal if the owner keeps adding labor and utilities.

## Diagnosis

The case combines three fatal errors:

- Official-channel failure: the user never verified the true official contact for the intended known brand and was diverted into a different brand.
- Product mismatch:养生奶茶 does not match school-gate impulse consumption, especially for younger students.
- Site illusion: "near school" is not demand proof when surrounding breakfast/stationery shops cannot survive and school rules may block consumption.

The employee is an immediate cash leak. At a few yuan to几十元 daily sales, paying someone to sit in the shop is not operating; it is extending loss.

## Recommendation Pattern

- Fire the employee immediately; owner alone watches the store if it must remain open.
- Do a short after-school observation test: record 10-15 minutes during dismissal to verify whether real student flow exists.
- If there is real flow, add low-price school-gate just-demand snacks such as fried potatoes, sausage, fire noodles, or simple炸货 to earn an owner's wage.
- Do not keep pushing养生饮品 as the main category.
- Contact the company immediately for refund/fee negotiation; organize contracts, payment records, site-selection promises,招商 claims, and service-delivery evidence.
- If negotiation fails, prepare complaint/litigation materials with professional legal support.

## Reusable Heuristics

- "学校旁边" is not enough; ask whether students can leave, when they pass, what they actually buy, and which nearby shops have survived.
- If monthly gross profit cannot cover utilities, labor must be removed before discussing marketing.
- A vulnerable family with only small savings should not borrow 200,000+ for an unverified franchise.
- "总部说适合" is not site due diligence; the user must verify local demand independently.
- If the user failed to find a known brand's official channel, reconstruct the contact path before analyzing store operation.
