## Calculation Logic

- Rent about 152/day + family labor counted about 400/day + utilities about 200/day = about 752/day.

- Because the family can self-operate and rent is low, this is not an immediate "close today" case if the model is simplified.

## Diagnosis

The store has two separate problems:

- Franchise/brand problem: the user thought he was entering a known category/brand lane but was redirected to a weak brand with no clear direct-store proof. A "brand" with no consumer recognition does not bring traffic.
- Operating/location problem: mall tail location plus over-complex equipment and menu makes a small-sales store carry unnecessary electricity and execution cost.

Unlike many cases, there is still a narrow self-rescue path because rent is low and the family can operate without hired staff.

## Recommendation Pattern

- Remove the weak brand sign/name; stop relying on it as a brand asset.
- Drop bad-selling砂锅/炒菜-like complexity. Focus on拌饭 as the base.
- Add simple adjacent staples like noodles, wontons, and dumplings so mall workers/customers have more just-demand choices.
- Reduce electricity cost by turning off or removing unnecessary high-energy equipment.
- Optimize delivery, but do not build the model on expensive menu complexity.
- Send termination/解除 notice to the company and preserve evidence.
- Report/complain through local market supervision or franchise-regulation channels if franchise filing/qualification/direct-store claims are unsupported.
- Try to recover agency/franchise fees and unused materials while continuing only the simplified self-owned model.

## Reusable Heuristics

- A company office/sample room is not a direct store.
- "A famous brand cannot join, so join our related/similar brand" is a high-risk diversion pattern.
- For weak mall-tail shops, inspect whether the store is on the real meal path or near dead-end facilities.
- If rent is low and owner/family labor can replace hired labor, simplify and rescue before closing.
- Expensive equipment can kill a small shop through energy cost even when rent is low.
